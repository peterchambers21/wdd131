Spay and Neuter

Spaying and neutering are safe, routine procedures that offer lifelong health benefits for your pet — and help reduce overpopulation in our communities. These surgeries can prevent certain cancers, reduce behavioral issues, and contribute to a longer, healthier life.
At Paws, Hooves, and Whiskers, we take every precaution to ensure your pet’s comfort and safety. From thorough pre-op evaluations to pain management and post-op care instructions, our team is here to support you and your animal every step of the way. Whether you're caring for a single rescue or managing a shelter, we’re here to help.
