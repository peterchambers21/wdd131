Check-Ups

Routine check-ups are essential to keeping your animals healthy and happy. During a standard wellness exam, we thoroughly assess your pet’s condition — checking weight, heart rate, temperature, joints, eyes, ears, skin, and more. It’s also the perfect time to ask questions and discuss your animal’s behavior, nutrition, or any concerns.
Preventive care helps us detect problems early and keep your pets and livestock on the right track. We offer vaccinations, deworming, dental checks, and tailored care plans for animals of all sizes. From puppies and kittens to senior animals and working livestock, regular exams are the cornerstone of lifelong health.
