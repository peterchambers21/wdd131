Overview

Purpose

A vet page that informs pet owners about services offered, provide care tips, and engage them through an appointment scheduler

Audience

Pet owners or anyone needing a vet

Dynamic elements

A schedualing page with a calander that allows you to see what days and times are avalible as well as book an appointment
A review page where people can submit and look at reviews
A contact page that lets people send a message to the company
